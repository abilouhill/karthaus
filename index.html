Bueler's Karthaus materials:
Numerical modelling of ice sheets, streams, and shelves

codes and lectures for  "Numerical modelling of ice sheets, streams, and shelves"  at
Karthaus 2012 Summer School on Ice Sheets and Glaciers in the Climate System, 11-22 September, 2012
all materials, including Matlab/Octave codes, petsc codes, and PDFs together:
         bueler_karthaus.zip       (19 Mb)
         bueler_karthaus.tar.gz   (19 Mb)

The above download is recommended, but here are the PDFs alone:
         notes.pdf  (5 Mb)     slides.pdf  (15 Mb)

individual Matlab/Octave codes:

  • heat and diffusion equation codes
    • heat.m
    • heatadapt.m
    • diffusion.m
  • SIA codes
    • halfar.m
    • halfarmovie.m   (depends: halfar.m)
    • siaflat.m   (depends: diffusion.m)
    • verifysia.m   (depends: halfar.m, siaflat.m, diffusion.m)
    • roughice.m    (depends: siaflat.m, diffusion.m)
    • siageneral.m  (depends: diffusion.m, getsurface.m)
  • SSA codes
    • flowline.m
    • testflowline.m   (depends: flowline.m)
    • convanalysis.m   (depends: testflowline.m, flowline.m)
    • ssainit.m
    • ssaflowline.m   (depends: ssainit.m, flowline.m)
    • exactshelf.m
    • testshelf.m   (depends: exactshelf.m, ssaflowline.m, ssainit.m, flowline.m)
    • shelfconv.m   (depends: testshelf.m, exactshelf.m, ssaflowline.m, ssainit.m, flowline.m)
  • Antarctic ice sheet data and tools:
    • Ant50km.nc  (50km gridded Antarctic data, sub-sampled from 5km NetCDF at SeaRISE assessment present day Antarctic data page)
    • netcdf.m  (NetCDF --> Matlab conversion; see netcdf_license.txt and http://www.mathworks.com/matlabcentral/fileexchange/15177)
    • buildant.m   (depends: netcdf.m, Ant50km.nc)
    • ant.m  (depends: buildant.m, siageneral.m, diffusion.m, netcdf.m, Ant50km.nc)
